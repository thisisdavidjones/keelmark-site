Privacy Policy

Keelmark products are designed to avoid unnecessary accounts, tracking, and data collection.

Summary

Dosewise is designed as a local-first Android app. Medication records are intended to remain on your device unless a future feature clearly states otherwise and asks for the relevant permission.

Information stored

Dosewise may store information you enter into the app, such as medication names, dose timing rules, and dose history. This information is used to provide the app's core functionality.

Analytics and diagnostics

If analytics or crash reporting are added, this policy should be updated to identify the service, the information collected, and how users can control it.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact hello@keelmark.co.uk.

This is a starter policy. Check it against the final app behaviour and Google Play requirements before publishing.
