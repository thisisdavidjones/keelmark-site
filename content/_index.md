        <h2>Small tools, deliberately kept small.</h2>
        <p>Keelmark products are intended to be practical, readable, and restrained. The point is to support a routine, not colonise it with dashboards, badges, streaks, and other ornamental nonsense.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="km-card-grid">
        <div class="km-card">
          <h3>Clear decisions</h3>
          <p>Each screen should help answer the immediate question in front of the user.</p>
        </div>
        <div class="km-card">
          <h3>Low friction</h3>
          <p>Common actions should be quick, obvious, and forgiving of normal mistakes.</p>
        </div>
        <div class="km-card">
          <h3>Local first</h3>
          <p>Where possible, personal routine data should stay on the device.</p>
