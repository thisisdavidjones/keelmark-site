Need help with a Keelmark app? Start here.

## Dosewise support

For Dosewise issues, please include:

- the app version;
- your Android version;
- your phone model;
- what you expected to happen;
- what actually happened;
- whether the issue happens every time or only sometimes.

## Contact

Email: [support@keelmark.co.uk](mailto:support@keelmark.co.uk)

## Before reporting a problem

A few things are worth checking first, because phones are tiny chaos rectangles and Android settings are where certainty goes to retire.

1. Check that notifications are enabled for the app.
2. Check that battery optimisation is not preventing reminders.
3. Confirm the medication schedule and daily limits are set correctly.
4. Restart the app after changing major settings.

## Medical safety

Dosewise is a reminder and logging tool. It does not provide medical advice, dosage recommendations, or clinical guidance. Always follow the instructions from your clinician, pharmacist, or medication label.
