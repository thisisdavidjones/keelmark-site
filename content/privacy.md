    <h1>Privacy Policy</h1>
    <p>Keelmark products are designed to avoid unnecessary accounts, tracking, and data collection.</p>
  </header>

  <section class="km-page-section">
    <h2>Summary</h2>
    <p>Dosewise is designed as a local-first Android app. Medication records are intended to remain on your device unless a future feature clearly states otherwise and asks for the relevant permission.</p>
  </section>

  <section class="km-page-section">
    <h2>Information stored</h2>
    <p>Dosewise may store information you enter into the app, such as medication names, dose timing rules, and dose history. This information is used to provide the app's core functionality.</p>
  </section>

  <section class="km-page-section">
    <h2>Analytics and diagnostics</h2>
    <p>If analytics or crash reporting are added, this policy should be updated to identify the service, the information collected, and how users can control it.</p>
