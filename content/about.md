  <header class="km-page-header">
    <p class="km-eyebrow">About</p>
    <h1>Practical software for ordinary routines.</h1>
    <p>Keelmark creates small Android apps that help people make clear decisions in everyday situations.</p>
  </header>

  <section class="km-page-section">
    <h2>What Keelmark builds</h2>
    <p>Keelmark products are designed around routine, repeatable tasks where a small amount of clarity can remove a surprising amount of uncertainty.</p>
    <p>The first product is Dosewise, a medication timing and dose logging app for Android. It focuses on recording what was taken, when it was taken, and when the next dose is due.</p>
  </section>

  <section class="km-page-section">
    <h2>How the apps are designed</h2>
    <div class="km-card-grid">
      <div class="km-card">
        <h3>Clarity first</h3>
        <p>An app should make the next useful action obvious. It should not require training, ritual, or interpretive dance.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="km-card">
        <h3>Privacy by default</h3>
        <p>Personal information should stay personal wherever possible. Keelmark apps are designed to avoid unnecessary accounts and unnecessary data collection.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="km-card">
        <h3>Focused features</h3>
        <p>A product should earn its place on your phone by solving a real problem well.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <section class="km-page-section">
    <h2>Maintenance</h2>
    <p>Software changes. Devices change. Android changes because apparently stillness is illegal. Keelmark aims to keep its apps maintained, documented, and understandable.</p>
  </section>
</div>
