  <header class="km-page-header">
    <p class="km-kicker">About</p>
    <h1>Practical Android apps for ordinary routines.</h1>
    <p>Keelmark creates small, reliable tools that help people make clear decisions in everyday situations.</p>
  </header>

  <section class="km-page-section">
    <h2>What Keelmark builds</h2>
    <p>Keelmark products focus on routine tasks where a little clarity can remove a surprising amount of uncertainty. The first product is Dosewise, a medication timing and dose logging app for Android.</p>
  </section>

  <section class="km-page-section">
    <h2>Design principles</h2>
    <div class="km-card-grid">
      <article class="km-card"><h3>Clarity first</h3><p>The next useful action should be obvious. A good app should not need a training course.</p></article>
      <article class="km-card"><h3>Privacy by default</h3><p>Personal information should stay personal wherever possible. Keelmark apps avoid unnecessary accounts, tracking, and data collection.</p></article>
      <article class="km-card"><h3>Focused features</h3><p>A product should earn its place on your phone by solving a real problem well.</p></article>
    </div>
  </section>

  <section class="km-page-section km-panel">
    <h2>Maintenance</h2>
    <p>Keelmark aims to keep its apps maintained, documented, and understandable as Android and devices change over time.</p>
  </section>
