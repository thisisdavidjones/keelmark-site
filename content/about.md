Keelmark creates practical Android apps for everyday routines.

The aim is simple: build small, reliable tools that help people make clear decisions without turning ordinary life into an admin platform. The first product, Dosewise, focuses on medication timing and dose logging.

## What we care about

Keelmark products are built around a few plain principles.

**Clarity first.** An app should make the next useful action obvious. It should not demand a training course, a ceremony, or a scented candle.

**Privacy by default.** Personal information should stay personal wherever possible. Keelmark apps are designed to avoid unnecessary accounts, unnecessary tracking, and unnecessary data collection.

**Focused features.** A product should earn its place on your phone. That means solving a real problem well, rather than collecting features like decorative clutter.

**Careful maintenance.** Software changes. Devices change. Android changes because apparently stillness is illegal. Keelmark aims to keep its apps maintained, documented, and straightforward to support.
