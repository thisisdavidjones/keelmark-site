Dosewise is being built to answer a simple question: **can I take this now?**

That sounds obvious, which is exactly why most software manages to make it slightly irritating. Dosewise focuses on medication timing, dose history, daily limits, and reminders that fit around real routines rather than pretending every day is a tidy diagram.
