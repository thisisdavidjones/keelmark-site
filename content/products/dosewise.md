      <h1>Medication timing and dose logging for Android.</h1>
      <p>Dosewise helps you record medication doses, check when the next dose is due, and keep a clear dose history.</p>
    </div>
  </header>

  <section class="km-page-section km-panel">
    <h2>What it is for</h2>
    <p>Medication can become so familiar that it is easy to forget whether you have just taken a dose. Dosewise is built around that ordinary problem: making the last recorded dose and next allowed dose easy to see.</p>
    <p class="km-note">Dosewise is not medical advice and does not decide what medication you should take. Medication instructions should come from your clinician, pharmacist, or the medicine label.</p>
  </section>

  <section class="km-page-section">
    <h2>Core features</h2>
    <div class="km-card-grid km-card-grid-3">
      <article class="km-card"><h3>Dose recording</h3><p>Record when a medication was taken and keep a history of previous doses.</p></article>
      <article class="km-card"><h3>Timing checks</h3><p>See whether enough time has passed before the next dose.</p></article>
      <article class="km-card"><h3>Medication setup</h3><p>Add medications with dose rules, daily limits, and schedule details.</p></article>
      <article class="km-card"><h3>Weekly routines</h3><p>Support for medicines taken on selected days rather than only daily intervals.</p></article>
      <article class="km-card"><h3>Readable history</h3><p>A clear record helps answer the question: what happened today?</p></article>
      <article class="km-card"><h3>Local first</h3><p>Designed to work without an unnecessary account.</p></article>
    </div>
  </section>
