    <p>Dosewise helps you record medication doses, check when the next dose is due, and keep a clear dose history.</p>

  <section class="km-page-section km-split">
    <div>
      <img class="km-product-art" src="/assets/dosewise-icon.svg" alt="Dosewise app icon">

    <div>
      <h2>What it is for</h2>
      <p>Medication can become so familiar that it is easy to forget whether you have just taken a dose. Dosewise is built around that very ordinary problem.</p>
      <p>The app is intended to help with routine tracking: what was taken, when it was taken, and what the timing rules say about the next dose.</p>
      <p class="km-note">Dosewise is not medical advice and does not decide what medication you should take. Medication instructions should come from your clinician, pharmacist, or the medicine label.</p>
    </div>
  </section>

  <section class="km-page-section">
    <h2>Core features</h2>
    <div class="km-card-grid">
      <div class="km-card">
        <h3>Dose recording</h3>
        <p>Record when a medication was taken and keep a history of previous doses.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="km-card">
        <h3>Timing checks</h3>
        <p>See whether enough time has passed before the next dose.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="km-card">
        <h3>Medication setup</h3>
        <p>Add medications with dose rules, daily limits, and schedule details.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="km-card">
        <h3>Weekly routines</h3>
        <p>Support for medicines taken on selected days rather than only daily intervals.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="km-card">
        <h3>Readable history</h3>
        <p>A clear record helps answer the question: what happened today?</p>
      </div>
      <div class="km-card">
        <h3>Local first</h3>
        <p>Designed to work without an unnecessary account. Add cloud features only if they earn their keep.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>
