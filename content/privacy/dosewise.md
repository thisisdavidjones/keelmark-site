Last updated: 27 June 2026

Dosewise is designed as a personal medication reminder and dose logging app.

## Information you enter

Dosewise may store information you enter about your medication schedule, dose history, reminder settings, and app preferences.

This information is used to provide the app’s core functions: reminders, dose timing, dose history, and schedule display.

## Where your data is stored

Dosewise is designed to store app data locally on your device. Keelmark does not require you to create an account to use Dosewise.

## Data sent to Keelmark

Dosewise is designed not to send your medication schedule or dose history to Keelmark.

If you contact support by email, Keelmark will receive the information you choose to include in that message. Please avoid sending sensitive medical information unless it is necessary for the support request.

## Advertising and tracking

Dosewise is designed without third-party advertising or behavioural tracking.

## Backups and exports

If Dosewise provides backup, import, or export features, any exported files are under your control. Take care when storing or sharing exported files, as they may contain personal medication information.

## Children

Dosewise is not aimed specifically at children. If a parent or guardian helps a child use the app, they are responsible for deciding what information is entered and how the app is used.

## Medical disclaimer

Dosewise is a reminder and logging tool. It is not a medical device, does not provide medical advice, and does not decide what medication you should take. Always follow the instructions from your clinician, pharmacist, or medication label.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact: [privacy@keelmark.co.uk](mailto:privacy@keelmark.co.uk)
